OUR SERVICES

The GOOD CARE Hospital Casualty Accident & Emergency Centre provides services 24 hours a day 7 days a week. It serves as an A&E Department, Out-Patient Department and as entry point for all the admission cases.

A well laid out emergency preparedness plan coupled with well-equipped staff has made the Centre a one-stop Centre able to handle mass accidents, acute cardio-respiratory problems, advanced trauma, gunshots, disaster management, poisons, and cases like malaria, pneumonia, typhoid fever, non-communicable and communicable diseases, and many others.

The A&E Department comprises of motivated team of doctors, nurses, technicians and other para health professionals well skilled in basic life support, advanced cardiac life support and trauma life support for adults, children and babies. Doctors and nurses undergo regular training to ensure their skills and knowledge is up to date.
